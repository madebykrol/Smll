Getting Started

When first getting familiar with Smll you are faced with a predefined directory structure starting at src/.
Smll is designed with conventions which bluntly forces you into a common way of structuring your source code.

Source structure

assets/

Common collection of all your layout material such as images and stylesheets

business/

All your buisness objects!

controllers/

Your Controllers by convention controller are namned <MyPage>Controller.php and with the same class name controllers must by convention either implement interface IController or extend abstract class Controller

helpers/

Static helper classes.

models/

Your MVC models.

modules/

Your or thirdparty plugins for Smll.

scripts/

Your scripts, commonly javascript files.

Views/

MVC Views
